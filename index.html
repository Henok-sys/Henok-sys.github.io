About Me

Hi! My name is Henok.

I am a Computer Science student at Williams College, class of 2026, with a passion for exploring the intersection of technology and user experience. As part of my studies, I took a Human-Computer Interaction course where I worked on several exciting projects, including developing a Chrome extension that encourages responsibility and integrating gesture recognition into the game Temple Run. Throughout these projects, I collaborated with teams and applied key principles of design, such as need finding, accessibility considerations, and design evaluation. This course gave me a deeper understanding of the iterative design process, from initial concept development to testing and refinement, and equipped me with the skills to create user-centered solutions.
